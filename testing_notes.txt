Game of Thrones Character Co-occurrence Network (Book 1)
=========================================================

── STEP 1: Analysis Tasks ─────────────────────────────────────────────────

Task 1:
  "Eddard Stark and Tyrion Lannister are both highly connected characters.
   Using the network, determine whether they share any direct connections
   with each other (i.e., do they appear in the same chapters?). Click
   each character's node or bar to inspect their neighborhoods."

Task 2:
  "Using the bar chart, identify the top 3 characters by co-occurrence
   weight. For each, click their bar to highlight their neighborhood.
   Which character has the most connections to characters outside their
   own house (i.e., their own color group)?"

── STEP 3: Adjustment Implemented ────────────────────────────────────────

Issue chosen:
  Small peripheral nodes were hard to click due to small hit area.

Adjustment made:
  Added a transparent larger click-area circle behind each node circle
  (radius = max(node_radius + 4, 10)) so that small nodes remain
  visually accurate but are easier to interact with. This is implemented
  as an invisible circle appended before the visible circle in each
  node group, sharing the same click and hover handlers.

  Additionally clarified the dimming behavior: updated the tooltip to
  say "Dimmed nodes = not directly connected" when a node is focused,
  so users understand what the visual encoding means.
